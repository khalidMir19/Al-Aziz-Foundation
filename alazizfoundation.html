About Us

Al-Aziz Foundation is based out of Badipora, Chadoora, Budgam, Jammu and Kashmir 191113. Our mission is to support widows, poor students who can't afford education, and individuals with serious medical issues. We strive to bring hope and relief to those in need.

Our Services

  • Financial assistance for widows.
  • Educational support for underprivileged students.
  • Medical aid for individuals with serious health conditions.
